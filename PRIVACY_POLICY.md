# Privacy Policy for TechnIQ

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025

## Introduction

TechnIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you create an account via Google Sign-In, we collect your name and email address
- **Player Profile**: Information you provide about your soccer training, including:
  - Age, position, experience level
  - Training goals and preferences
  - Self-identified skill areas for improvement
- **Training Data**: Information about your training sessions, including:
  - Session dates, duration, and intensity
  - Exercises completed and performance ratings
  - Progress tracking and skill assessments

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Usage Analytics**: How you interact with the app features (anonymized)
- **Error Logs**: Technical information to help us fix bugs and improve performance

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide personalized soccer training recommendations
- Track your progress and generate performance insights
- Sync your data across devices via Firebase
- Improve our machine learning recommendations
- Fix bugs and enhance app performance
- Communicate important updates about the app

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication)
- We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest
- Local data is stored on your device using Core Data with appropriate protections
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

## Third-Party Services

TechnIQ uses the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Authentication**: For secure user authentication via Google Sign-In
- **Firebase Firestore**: For cloud data storage and synchronization
- **Firebase Functions**: For server-side processing and ML recommendations
- **YouTube Data API**: To fetch training video recommendations (no YouTube data is stored)

Each service has its own privacy policy:
- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms)

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: Request a copy of your personal information
- **Delete Your Data**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Update Your Information**: Modify your profile information at any time through the app
- **Opt-Out**: Disconnect your account and stop using the service

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [evan10takahashi@gmail.com].

## Data Retention

- We retain your data while your account is active
- If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
