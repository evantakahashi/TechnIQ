Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account data: email address, name, profile information
  • Training data: exercises, sessions, ratings, progress, match logs
  • Usage data: app interactions, feature usage for improving recommendations

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized AI training plans and recommendations
  • Track your progress and generate analytics
  • Sync your data across devices via cloud backup
  • Improve our AI models and app experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • YouTube Data API: Video drill recommendations
  • OpenAI / Vertex AI: AI-powered training plan and drill generation

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device (Core Data) and in the cloud (Google Firebase/Firestore). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View your data within the App
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data via Settings
  • Portability: Your training data is accessible through the App

7. Children's Privacy

TechnIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at privacy@techniq-app.com.
